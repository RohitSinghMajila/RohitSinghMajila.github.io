HR Analytics Dashboard


In my HR Analytics dashboard created using Tableau, I have successfully crafted a comprehensive visualization of critical workforce metrics. This visualization allows stakeholders to swiftly gauge overall job satisfaction trends and identify potential areas for improvement.


Furthermore, my dashboard presents a detailed analysis of Attrition within the organization. Through interactive filters, users can dissect the attrition data by department and gender, revealing patterns that inform targeted retention strategies. This segment is designed to help HR teams pinpoint departments or demographics that may require focused attention to reduce attrition rates effectively.


In essence, my HR Analytics dashboard in Tableau consolidates these crucial metrics into an accessible and insightful interface, empowering HR professionals and organizational leaders with actionable insights to enhance employee satisfaction, mitigate attrition, and strategically manage their workforce for success.
