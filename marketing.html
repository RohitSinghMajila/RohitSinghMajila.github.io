Marketing Analytics


The marketing analytics dashboard, built using Power BI, provides an in-depth analysis of campaign performance and customer engagement. Key performance indicators (KPIs) such as Clicks, Engagement, Impressions, Click-Through Rate (CTR), Engagement Rate, Bids Distribution, and Spend are central to the dashboard, delivering actionable insights into marketing effectiveness. Clicks and Impressions help understand the reach and initial engagement of campaigns, while CTR, the ratio of Clicks to Impressions, indicates the percentage of users engaging with the content. Engagement Rate measures the depth of interaction, assessing the quality of engagement.


The dashboard also presents Bids Distribution, offering insights into how marketing bids are allocated across channels and campaigns to ensure optimal spend. Spend tracks the overall investment in marketing activities, allowing for an evaluation of ROI. By visualizing these KPIs, the dashboard supports data-driven decision-making, identifying high-performing strategies and areas for improvement. Overall, it offers a detailed overview of key metrics, enhancing the efficiency and effectiveness of marketing initiatives.
